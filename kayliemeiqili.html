ABOUT ME.

My name is Kaylie Meiqi Li, and I am current undergraduate student at University of Southern California, Iovine and Young Academy studying Arts, Technology, and the Business of Innovation.✌️

When I am not chasing the sunset, I am pursuing my ambitions to be an aspiring sustainable entrepreneur. I am interested in building long-term innovation from the ground up that will have a sustainable impact on the communities around.
